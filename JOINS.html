21. Display department with maximum employees.
SELECT D.DNAME, COUNT(E.EMPNO) AS EMP_COUNT 
FROM SCOTT.DEPT D 
JOIN SCOTT.EMP E ON D.DEPTNO = E.DEPTNO 
GROUP BY D.DNAME 
HAVING COUNT(E.EMPNO) = (SELECT MAX(COUNT(*)) FROM SCOTT.EMP GROUP BY DEPTNO);
Output:

SALES6
